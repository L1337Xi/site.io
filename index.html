About our College

Established in 2012, M Dasan Institute of Technology (MDIT) stands at the forefront of innovation in engineering education under the M. Dasan Memorial Co‑operative Institute of Engineering and Information Technology . Rooted in the co-operative sector, MDIT upholds a culture of transparency, participation, and shared growth, reflecting our philosophy that education thrives where people, purpose, and progress come together.  Located amidst the serene greenery of Ulliyeri, Kozhikode, our campus offers an environment that inspires focused learning and holistic development. Approved by the AICTE and affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (APJAKTU), MDIT offers eight undergraduate engineering programs across six departments.
